Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a pet?

Tap the "+" button on the main Pets screen. Fill in your pet's information including name, species, breed, date of birth, and photo. You can also add weight and notes.

How do I schedule medications?

Open your pet's detail page, tap "Add Medication" in the Medications section. Enter the medication name, dosage, and instructions. Then add schedule times with your preferred frequency (daily, weekly, custom). You must specify how many times or until when the schedule should repeat.

Can I track grooming and other care tasks?

Yes! When adding a medication, toggle the type to "Care Task" instead of "Medication". You can schedule grooming, nail trimming, ear cleaning, baths, flea treatments, and more.

Why do I need to set an end condition for schedules?

To prevent notification overflow and ensure your device isn't cluttered with unlimited reminders, all recurring schedules must have either a repeat count (e.g., 30 times) or an end date. This is a design feature to protect your notification system.

How do I mark a medication as taken?

Go to the Reminders tab, find the upcoming reminder, and tap "Mark Taken". The app will warn you if you're marking it too early or too late, or if it's already been taken today.

How do I generate a pet sitter report?

Open your pet's detail page and tap "Pet Sitter Report". Select which pets to include, choose your date range, and tap "Generate Report". You can then preview and share the report via text or email.

Can I track weight for my pets?

Yes! On your pet's detail page, scroll to the Weight History section. Tap "Add Weight Entry", enter the weight with your preferred unit (kg, lb, or g), date, and optional notes.

How do I schedule vet visits?

On your pet's detail page, scroll to the Upcoming Vet Visits section. Tap "Add Vet Visit", select the date and time, and add an optional reason. You'll receive reminders 24 hours before the appointment.

Can I delete medications or care tasks?

Yes! Swipe left on any medication or care task in your pet's detail page. A confirmation dialog will appear before deletion. All associated schedules and history will be removed.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your data is stored locally on your device only. Mascotty never uploads your data to servers or shares it with third parties. Your pets' health information stays completely private.

How do I change the app language?

The app uses your device's language settings. Go to your iPhone Settings > General > Language & Region, and change your preferred language. The app will automatically adapt.

Can I use Mascotty offline?

Yes! Mascotty works completely offline. All features are available without an internet connection since no data is synced to the cloud.
