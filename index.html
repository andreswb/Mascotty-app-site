Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

Free: 1 pet, up to 2 care tasks, unlimited medications, weight tracking, health records.

Pro (One-Time Purchase): Unlimited pets, unlimited care tasks, vet visit tracking, pet sitter reports.

No subscriptions! Pay once and own forever.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

When you try to add a second pet, third care task, create a pet sitter report, or add a vet visit, the app will show you the upgrade screen. You can also upgrade from Settings > Upgrade to Pro.

How do I add a pet?

Tap the "+" button on the main Pets screen. Fill in your pet's information including name, species, breed, date of birth, and photo. You can also add weight and notes. Free users can add 1 pet, Pro users can add unlimited pets.

How do I schedule medications?

Open your pet's detail page, tap "Add Medication" in the Medications section. Enter the medication name, dosage, and instructions. Then add schedule times with your preferred frequency (daily, weekly, custom). You must specify how many times or until when the schedule should repeat.

Can I track grooming and other care tasks?

Yes! When adding a medication, toggle the type to "Care Task" instead of "Medication". You can schedule grooming, nail trimming, ear cleaning, baths, flea treatments, and more. Free users can track up to 2 care tasks, Pro users have unlimited care tasks.

How do I schedule vet visits?

Vet visit tracking is a Pro feature. On your pet's detail page, scroll to the Upcoming Vet Visits section. Tap "Add Vet Visit", select the date and time, and add an optional reason. You'll receive reminders 24 hours before the appointment.

How do I generate a pet sitter report?

Pet sitter reports are a Pro feature. Open your pet's detail page and tap "Pet Sitter Report" or go to Settings. Select which pets to include, choose your date range (maximum 90 days), and tap "Generate Report". You can then preview and share the report via text or email.

Why do I need to set an end condition for schedules?

To prevent notification overflow and ensure your device isn't cluttered with unlimited reminders, all recurring schedules must have either a repeat count (e.g., 30 times) or an end date. This is a design feature to protect your notification system.

How do I mark a medication as taken?

Go to the Reminders tab, find the upcoming reminder, and tap "Mark Taken" (for medications) or "Mark Completed" (for care tasks). The app will warn you if you're marking it too early or too late, or if it's already been taken today.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your data is stored locally on your device only. Mascotty never uploads your data to servers or shares it with third parties. Your pets' health information stays completely private.
